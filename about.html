Our Story

Traquiva was born from a simple observation: traditional tech education is broken. Bootcamps cost $15,000+, online courses lack personalization, and universities teach outdated technologies.

In 2024, our founders—former engineers from Google, Meta, and Amazon—decided to build something different. They combined their expertise in education, AI, and software development to create a platform that makes learning accessible, personalized, and effective.

Today, Traquiva serves over 10,000 students worldwide, helping them transition into tech careers and achieve their dreams.

Our Mission

To democratize tech education by providing affordable, AI-powered learning experiences that prepare students for real-world careers.

Our Vision

A world where anyone, anywhere can access world-class tech education and build a successful career in technology.

Our Values

Accessibility, quality, innovation, community, and results. We're committed to helping every student succeed.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional tech education has three major problems that prevent people from succeeding.

Too Expensive

Bootcamps cost $15,000-$20,000. That's a huge financial barrier that excludes millions of talented people from entering tech.

Outdated Content

Universities and many online courses teach technologies that companies stopped using years ago. Students graduate unprepared.

No Personalization

One-size-fits-all curricula don't work. Everyone learns differently, but traditional education treats all students the same.

Our Approach

We've built a platform that solves these problems through technology and innovation.

AI-Powered Personalization

Our AI analyzes your learning style, pace, and goals to create a personalized curriculum that adapts as you progress.

Industry-Standard Projects

Build real applications using the latest technologies that companies actually use. Create a portfolio that gets you hired.

Expert Mentorship

Learn from developers at top tech companies. Get code reviews, career advice, and personalized guidance.

Affordable Pricing
