Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes! Pro comes with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students get 50% off Pro plans with a valid .edu email address. Contact support@traquiva.com to apply.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund—no questions asked.

How do mentor sessions work?

Book 1-on-1 video sessions with expert developers. Sessions are 30-60 minutes and can be used for code reviews, career advice, or technical questions.
